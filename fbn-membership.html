Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Individual and Team?

Individual is for one person — great for independent professionals, consultants, or anyone joining on their own. Team is for companies who want to bring multiple people into the network — choose 5 or 15 seats depending on your team size. Both get the same core community benefits.

What does "founding member rate locked in forever" mean?

The price you pay now will never increase for as long as you maintain your membership. As the network grows and standard prices go up, your rate stays the same.

How limited is the $99 individual intro pricing?

We're offering $99 to a limited number of founding individual members. Once those spots are filled, the price goes to $299/year. We recommend locking it in early.

When does the network officially launch?

We're accepting founding members now! The official launch — including launch parties — is planned for January 2027. Founding members get immediate Slack access and will be the first invited to all events.

Is this an annual membership?

Yes, all memberships are billed annually. Your founding rate renews at the same price each year.

Is there a refund policy?

All membership fees are non-refundable. We encourage you to review the membership benefits and reach out with any questions before joining to make sure it's the right fit.

How do sponsorship and partnership packages work?

Sponsorship packages include team memberships, event access, branding, and content opportunities. Each tier has fixed pricing and clearly defined benefits — see the packages above, or reach out for details.
